Introduction

Our aim through education and engagement was to broaden the accessibility of synthetic biology, particularly reaching out to communities with limited exposure or learning opportunities in this field. Our objectives are in harmony with two of the UN Sustainable Development Goals, Quality Education and Reduced Inequalities, aiming to foster a more prosperous and sustainable future.

Undergraduate Students

The UVA Engineering Summer Bridge Program is hosted by UVA’s Center for Diversity in Engineering to ease the transition to college for incoming first-year engineering students. On July 19, several of our team members gave a talk to Bridge program participants about synthetic biology. We discussed our iGEM project this year and encouraged them to apply in the fall semester to be part of next year’s team!
