What is BLISS?

The Bacillus Lipase Integrated Secretion System (BLISS) is a groundbreaking probiotic developed to address Exocrine Pancreatic Insufficiency (EPI). Through genetic engineering, B. subtilis has been modified to produce human pancreatic lipases, procolipases, amylases, and proteases, functioning as a pancreatic enzyme replacement therapy.
